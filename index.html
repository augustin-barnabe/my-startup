Our idea :

Our idea was to create this platform so that you, the customer, can choose the best possible recipe for your meal. We will ask you for differents ingredients to create a new recipe for us with your name.

How to do?

TWe offer you different ideas of dishes that we are currently preparing. You will have the choice with new ingredients, sauces and accompaniments. You will have to create the recipe from A to Z so that we can see the possibility of doing it. It would have to use exactly the machines that we have in order to make it. You will have a free menu for 1 month if your recipe is selected
